Questions without answers.

This chapter gave you one tool and one technique. Before moving on, sit with these questions.

Consider this
  • › QUESTION 1 — points at a constraint or assumption the chapter rested on.
  • › QUESTION 2 — asks what would change if a variable were different.
  • › QUESTION 3 — references an alternative tool or approach that hasn't been covered yet.
  • › QUESTION 4 — asks about ordering, timing, or context-dependence.

You don't need answers right now. The point is to recognize the edges of what you know, and to understand that those gaps are exactly worth exploring.
